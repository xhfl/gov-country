South Korea Country Project - Government: Executive

Executive Branch

South Korea

  • Chief of State: President Moon Jae-In (문재인)
  • Head of Government: Prime Minister Lee Nak-yon (이낙욘)
  • Cabinet: State Council appointed by president on prime minister’s recommendation
  • Elections: President directly elected by a simple majority popular vote for a 5-year term, Prime Minister appointed by president with consent of National Assembly

United States

  • Chief of State: President Donald Trump
  • Head of government: President Donald Trump
  • Cabinet: Presidential Cabinet appointed by President, approved by Senate
  • Elections: President elected by electoral college, serving 4-year terms
